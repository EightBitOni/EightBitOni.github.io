## Jeaw "Thai Dipping sauce"

![Jeaw](<../../assets/images/Pasted image 20220712024110.png>)

### Dipping Sauce

- 3 tsp fish sauce
- 2 tsp lime juice
- 2.5 tsp sugar
- 1-2 tsp chili
- 2 tsp rice powder
- 1 tbs cilantro
